# Creativity Engine · 创造力引擎

A civilisation-scale, fully bilingual (EN · 中文) platform exploring the nature of creativity as **recombination** — how invention, art, mathematics, science, memes, play, collective intelligence and AI all generate novelty by rearranging existing patterns into structures that did not exist before.

> Creativity is the process by which intelligence generates new realities from existing structures.
>
> 创造力，是智能从既有结构中生成新现实的过程。

A global language toggle switches every label, diagram, graph, timeline and essay between English and Chinese without a reload.

## The ten systems

1. **The Origin of the Creative Mind** — tool use, play, language and symbolic cognition assemble a novelty-generating engine
2. **The Idea Recombination Engine** — interactive fusion graph: a creation's source patterns merge into a new whole
3. **The Artistic Creation Engine** — an influence tree of art movements, each inheriting a vocabulary and breaking one rule
4. **Scientific & Technological Creativity** — a discovery network where one field's idea becomes another's instrument
5. **Mathematics & Abstract Creation** — a discovered-vs-invented toggle over primes, π, √−1, non-Euclidean geometry, infinity
6. **Memes & Internet Creativity** — a memetic mutation tree running variation-and-selection at network speed
7. **The Childhood & Play Engine** — pretend, exploration, improvisation and experiment as the rehearsal of invention
8. **The Collective Intelligence System** — a civilisation-scale idea network from Athens to open source
9. **AI & Synthetic Creativity** — a human-vs-AI comparator across the dimensions of creative work
10. **The Future Creation Engine** — human–AI co-creation, generative worlds, autonomous invention, the curation crisis

Plus a **Creativity-Capacity** meta-model: a seven-axis radar scoring each creation on pattern recognition, symbolic recombination, abstraction, simulation depth, cross-domain transfer, emotional modelling, and novelty generation.

Featured creations, dissected: the airplane, the printing press, Cubism, calculus, jazz, the blockchain, the transistor, cinema, imaginary numbers, and the internet meme — each shown as the source patterns it recombined, the genuine creative leap, and its impact on civilisation, with a procedural symbolic glyph.
